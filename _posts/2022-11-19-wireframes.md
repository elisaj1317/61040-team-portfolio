# Wireframe Screenshots
<figure class="align-center">
  <img src="/images/wireframes/account_page.png" class="align-center" style="width:90%;">
  <figcaption>A user posts listings of soon-to-expire items to their home community. Other users contact this user through the listed contact information when they would like a listed item. The suggested recipes are tailored to the allergies and dietary restrictions listed on this page.</figcaption>
</figure>

<figure class="align-center">
  <img src="/images/wireframes/stockpile.png" class="align-center" style="width:90%;">
  <figcaption>The stockpile contains all the items in the user’s fridge or pantry. The stockpile sorts ingredients by the expiry date and clearly notifies the user of expired items so the user can throw them away.</figcaption>
</figure>

<figure class="align-center">
  <img src="/images/wireframes/add_listing.png" class="align-center" style="width:90%;">
  <figcaption>The user can click “Create listing” to bring up a “New listing” form. This form will contain information such as price, relevant to other users who want to purchase and pick up the item.</figcaption>
</figure>

<figure class="align-center">
  <img src="/images/wireframes/your_listings.png" class="align-center" style="width:90%;">
  <figcaption>Users can add items to their listings. These items can be soon-to-expire foods or items they have too much of. When other users search for recipes and they do not have an ingredient, this user’s listing can pop up as an option.</figcaption>
</figure>

<figure class="align-center">
  <img src="/images/wireframes/quick_suggest.png" class="align-center" style="width:90%;">
  <figcaption>FridgeCheck provides users with the quick option to find recipes using any of the ingredients on-hand and to search for a particular snack or meal they have in mind. The results of either search brings up recipe suggestions</figcaption>
</figure>

<figure class="align-center">
  <img src="/images/wireframes/selected_recipe.png" class="align-center" style="width:90%;">
  <figcaption>If the user selects a particular suggested recipe, FridgeCheck informs the user of which ingredients they have and which can be found on listings. The suggested listings contain contact information to reach out to someone for the necessary ingredient.</figcaption>
</figure>

<figure class="align-center">
  <img src="/images/wireframes/communities.png" class="align-center" style="width:90%;">
  <figcaption>A user can follow other communities to view listings from the dorms they are willing to walk to pick up items from.</figcaption>
</figure>
